Policies:
1. Pre-Shipment Cancellation: Cancellation is only allowed if the order status is "Pending."
2. Time-Based Cancellation Window: If an order was placed less than 10 days ago, it is eligible for cancellation. Otherwise, cancellation is not permitted.
3. No Cancellation for Certain Items that are perishable or digital. eg:  food, flowers, digital downloads.
